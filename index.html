Flagship Initiative

Founder of Sananka Fashion Festival

Sananka is the founder and visionary behind Sananka Fashion Festival, an annual platform celebrating the synergy between fashion, culture, and tourism.

The festival positions Laikipia as a thriving creative hub by connecting designers, models, artists, tourism voices, and industry stakeholders in one purpose-driven ecosystem.
